# Living Will Assistance for Jehovah's Witnesses

An interactive, single-file HTML tool to help Jehovah's Witnesses document their medical decisions in a structured, printable format — ready to be entered into the official **dpa** advance directive. The tool takes into account the new guidance from the “2026 Governing Body Update #2”.

---

## What This Is

Completing an advance directive as a Jehovah's Witness involves more than simply refusing blood transfusions. It requires carefully considered, individually documented decisions across a range of medical procedures — from autologous blood techniques and blood fractions to life-sustaining measures across different clinical scenarios.

This tool walks through each category step by step, lets you record your personal decisions, and generates a ready-to-use text for **Section 3** of the dpa form.

---

## Features

- **Four categories of decisions**
  - Allogeneic blood (fixed refusals — not a conscience matter)
  - Autologous blood procedures (haemodilution, cell salvage, heart-lung machine, dialysis, plasmapheresis, blood labelling, epidural blood patch)
  - Blood fractions (albumin, immunoglobulins, clotting factors, haemoglobin-based oxygen carriers, EPO, PRP)
  - Life-sustaining measures (mechanical ventilation, CPR, artificial nutrition, ICU, palliative care, life-prolonging medications) — each evaluated across **three clinical scenarios**

- **Three clinical scenarios for life-sustaining measures**
  - S1: Temporarily curable (e.g. accident, surgical complication)
  - S2: Permanently unconscious (e.g. persistent vegetative state)
  - S3: Terminal phase (end-stage incurable illness)

- **Generates form-ready text** for Section 3 of the dpa-X Ge directive — copy and paste directly

- **Four languages** — German 🇩🇪 · English 🇬🇧 · Spanish 🇪🇸 · French 🇫🇷

- **Dark and Light mode**

- **Zero dependencies** — single HTML file, works offline, no data is sent anywhere

---

## How to Use

1. Download `living-will-helper-for-jw.html`
2. Open it in any modern browser — no installation, no server needed
3. Work through each section and toggle your decisions
4. Click **"Text for Directive"** to generate your personalised text
5. Copy the text and enter it into Section 3 of your dpa form
6. Review the completed form with your **eldest** before signing

---

## Important Notes

> This tool is a **decision-making aid**, not a legally binding document. The generated text is designed to complement the official dpa-X Ge form and is not a substitute for it.

- All decisions on autologous procedures and blood fractions are **individual conscience matters** — this tool presents the facts, not recommendations
- The fixed refusals (whole blood and the four primary components) reflect the clear Scriptural position (Acts 15:28, 29).
- Before finalising your directive, consult your congregation's **eldests** — they can review the wording and ensure it is legally effective in your country
- Medical translations for ES and FR are accurate but should be verified by someone with local medical and legal knowledge before use in a non-German-speaking country

## Privacy

No data ever leaves your device. The tool runs entirely in the browser with no external API calls, no analytics, and no storage beyond your own browser session. There is nothing to log in to and nothing to install.
