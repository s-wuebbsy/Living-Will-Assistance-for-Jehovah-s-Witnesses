# Living Will Assistance for Jehovah's Witnesses

An interactive, single-file HTML tool to help Jehovah's Witnesses document their medical decisions in a structured, printable format — generating a text suggestion for the official **Dokument zur ärztlichen Versorgung** (advance directive). The tool takes into account the guidance from the [2026 Governing Body Update #2](https://www.jw.org/de/bibliothek/videos/#en/mediaitems/StudioFeatured/docid-1112024049_1_VIDEO).

---

## What This Is

Completing an advance directive as a Jehovah's Witness involves more than simply refusing blood transfusions. It requires carefully considered, individually documented decisions across a range of medical procedures — from autologous blood techniques and blood fractions to life-sustaining measures across different clinical scenarios.

This tool walks through each category step by step, lets you record your personal decisions, and generates a **text suggestion** that can serve as a basis for your advance directive.

---

## Features

- **Five categories of decisions**
  - **Allogeneic blood** — refusals, not a conscience matter (Acts 15:28, 29)
  - **Autologous blood procedures** — haemodilution, cell salvage, heart-lung machine, dialysis, plasmapheresis, blood labelling, epidural blood patch
  - **Blood fractions / derivatives** — albumin, immunoglobulins, clotting factors, haemoglobin-based oxygen carriers, EPO, PRP — with info on vaccine use and synthetic variants
  - **Life-sustaining measures** — mechanical ventilation, CPR, artificial nutrition, ICU, palliative care, life-prolonging medications — each evaluated across three clinical scenarios
  - **Blood-sparing alternatives** — volume expanders, iron infusions, minimal blood sampling

- **Three clinical scenarios for life-sustaining measures**
  - S1: Good prospect of recovery (e.g. after accident or surgical complication)
  - S2: No prospect of improvement (e.g. persistent vegetative state, severe brain damage)
  - S3: Terminal phase (end-stage incurable illness)

- **Text suggestion** — generates a draft text for your advance directive, clearly framed as a suggestion to review with a congregation elder

- **Four languages** — German, English, Spanish, French

- **Dark and Light mode**

- **Persistent decisions** — your choices, language, and colour mode are saved in your browser (localStorage) and restored automatically

- **Progress indicator** — shows how many decisions have been made

- **Reset** — clear all decisions with one click

- **Print-friendly** — dedicated print stylesheet for clean output

- **Accessible** — ARIA roles, focus management, keyboard navigation, screen reader support

- **Zero dependencies** — single HTML file, works offline, no data is sent anywhere

## How to Use

1. Download `living-will-helper-for-jw.html`
2. Open it in any modern browser — no installation, no server needed
3. Work through each section and toggle your decisions
4. Click **"Text for Directive"** to generate your personalised text suggestion
5. Review the suggestion and adapt it as needed for your advance directive
6. If you are unsure about any procedure or its wording, speak to one of your **congregation elders**

---

## Important Notes

> This tool is a **decision-making aid**, not a legally binding document. The generated text is a suggestion and is not a substitute for the official advance directive.

- All decisions on autologous procedures and blood fractions are **individual conscience matters** — this tool presents the facts, not recommendations
- The refusals of whole blood and the four primary components reflect the clear Scriptural position (Acts 15:28, 29)
- If you are unsure about anything, speak to one of your **congregation elders** — they can help review the wording and ensure it is appropriate
- Medical translations for ES and FR are accurate but should be verified by someone with local medical and legal knowledge before use in a non-German-speaking country

---

## Privacy

No data is sent anywhere. The tool runs entirely in the browser with no external API calls and no analytics. Your decisions are stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and never leave your device. There is nothing to log in to and nothing to install.
