# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This project serves a specific religious community and deals with deeply personal medical decisions. We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful environment for everyone — regardless of background, experience level, or personal beliefs.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions
- Focus feedback on the tool, its accuracy, and its usability
- Respect that medical and conscience decisions are deeply personal
- Welcome corrections — whether medical, legal, linguistic, or technical

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Disrespectful comments about religious beliefs or practices
- Deliberately introducing medical misinformation
- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language
- Spam or off-topic promotion

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces: issues, pull requests, discussions, and any related communication channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour can be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer directly. Reports will be reviewed and addressed promptly. The maintainer reserves the right to remove content or block participants who violate this code of conduct.
